Golden Krown v.2.0

Basics

Move elements on the field by selecting an element and pointing a target to move to. Make vertical, horizontal or diagonal lines of 5 or more same color elements to destroy them and make scores. Do not let field be full of elements or you'll loose.

Scores

Each complete line will give you a score elements of that color. To increment your score value you must archive score elements of each of 6 spectrum colors. They will collapse and give you one real score. Archive score value grater, than current King have to earn a Golden Krown and bocome King yourself.

Bonuses

Complete lines with more than 5 elements to get bonuses
  • Collapse 6 elements of the same color or all 6 colors to get a Universal Matcher. Use it to match any of possible color to complete a line.
  • Collapse 7 elements of the same color to get a Vertical Line Feed, Horizontal Line Feed or Explosive Element.
    • Use Vertical Line Feed to destroy vertical line of elements.
    • Use Horizontal Line Feed to destroy horizontal line of elements.
    • Use Explosive Element to destroy all elements around it.
  • Collapse 8 elements of the same color to get a Spectrum Nova. Use it to destroy all elements of selected color.
  • Collapse 9 elements of the same color to get a Black Hole. Use it to destroy everything and everyone.
